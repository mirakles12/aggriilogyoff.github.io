About us

AgriLogy is a company specializing in the agricultural field, especially irrigation services and products we help farmers and gardeners to
irrigate and monitor their crops with fewer resources and efforts, with higher yields. We have an extensive collection of products and services such:


*Products:
-Smart irrigation and watering system based on IoT technology.
- Irrigation devices, and accessories .


*Services:
-Maintenance and installation of smart irrigation (IoT).
-Installation and Maintenance of solar pumping system.
